How to Read Guide

  • “Get” means it is a quest reward.
  • “Buy” means you purchase it from town vendor.
  • Links and sockets are separated meaning if something needs to be linked it will have a “+” and in its own line, otherwise it will be separated by a comma and you can socket the single gem anywhere it fits on gear.
  • If you see a “=” in one of the links, that means it is an alternate link.
    For example,     Link 1: Sunder + Melee Phys + Close Combat = Ruthless
  • This means if you find R R G you can use Close Combat 
    or if you find     R R R use Ruthless

ACT 1 Progression

End of Act 1: 20-23 mins

Level 12-13

Starting gems: Heavy Strike + Ruthless

Ensure you have RR linked somewhere before leaving town

Town

  • Get Ground Slam (left gem) > Replace Heavy Strike
  • Buy Stone Axe
  • Buy any R-R linked
  • Pick up any shield and put on offhand for Shield Charge at lvl 4

Hailrake

  • Get Quicksilver
  • Get Decoy Totem [optional]
  • Get Shield Charge
  • Get Chance to Bleed or Momentum
  • Buy Jade Amulet/Iron Ring [3 Wisdom each]
  • Buy Frostblink [Wisdom]
  • Buy Momentum or Chance to Bleed [Wisdom]
  • Buy Blood & Sand [Wisdom]

Dweller

  • Get Faster Attack

Brutus

  • Get Leap Slam
  • Buy Intimidating Cry [Trans]

Fairgraves

  • Get Sunder
  • Get Woodsplitter if lvl 13

LINKS

1-11

  • Link 1: Ground Slam + Ruthless + Bleed = Momentum

At 12 Mervail

  • Link 1: Sunder + Ruthless + Bleed

  • Link 2: Leap + FA + Momentum
  • Util: Frostblink, Blood & Sand, Int Cry
  • Optional: Decoy

Cost: 1 Trans, 3+ Wisdoms
