# 3.27 Sunder Leveling into Pohx RF Swap
Updated: Feb 11, 2026

<div class="note-block">
<ul class="custom-list bullet-list">

- This guide adapts <a href="https://pohx.net/">Pohx's RF guide</a> by using Sunder for leveling instead of the traditional caster approach. Play Sunder until Level 52-55, then use the free campaign respec and gold at Blood Aqueducts to hard-swap into Righteous Fire and follow Pohx's instructions. This approach allows you to level significantly quicker while still transitioning smoothly into the powerful RF setup once you reach maps.
  
</ul>
</div>

## <span id="a1"></span>ACT 1 PROGRESSION
### End of Act 1: 20-23 mins - Level 12

<ul class="custom-list bullet-list">

- Starting Gems: [Heavy Strike](gemred)+[Ruthless](gemred)
- Ensure you have [R-R](gemred) linked somewhere before leaving town
  
</ul>

<div class="note-block">
<ul class="custom-list bullet-list">

- Below are all breakpoints by monster, after each name go to town and get the items listed.
  
</ul>


</div>

### Tarkleigh
<ul class="checklist">

- [ ] Get [Ground Slam](gemred)(left gem) → Replace [Heavy Strike](gemred)
- [ ] Buy [Stone Axe](gemgrey) for cheap
- [ ] Buy any [R-R](gemred) linked
- [ ] Pick up any shield and put it on off-hand for [Shield Charge](gemred) at level 4 

</ul>

### Hailrake
<ul class="checklist">

- [ ] Get [Quicksilver](gemgrey)
- [ ] Get [Decoy Totem](gemred) (optional)
- [ ] Get [Shield Charge](gemred)
- [ ] Get [Chance to Bleed](gemred) or [Momentum](gemgreen) 
- I personally take [Momentum](gemgreen) and link with [Shield Charge](gemred)
- [ ] Buy [Jade Amulet / Iron Ring](gemgrey) 3x <img src="images/wisdom.png" class="icon-inline">
- [ ] Buy [Frostblink](gemblue) <img src="images/wisdom.png" class="icon-inline">
- [ ] Buy [Momentum](gemgreen) or [Chance to Bleed](gemred)<img src="images/wisdom.png" class="icon-inline">
- Buy the other gem you didn't get earlier
- [ ] Buy [Blood & Sand](gemred)<img src="images/wisdom.png" class="icon-inline">

</ul>

### Dweller
<ul class="checklist">

- [ ] Get [Faster Attacks](gemgreen)
- [ ] Buy 1-2x [Iron Rings](gemgrey) and 2x [mana potions](gemblue) to make Brutus fight easier

</ul>

### Brutus
<ul class="checklist">

- [ ] Get [Leap Slam](gemred)
- [ ] Get [Indimidating Cry](gemred)

</ul>

### Fairgraves (Enter Caverns)
<ul class="checklist">

- [ ] Get [Sunder](gemred)

</ul>

<div class="link-block" id="#a1links">
<ul class="checklist">

### Level 1-9: Use Ground Slam
- [ ] Link 1: [Ground Slam](gemred)+[Ruthless](gemred)+[Chance to Bleed](gemred) or [Momentum](gemgreen)
- [ ] Link 2: [Shield Charge](gemred)+[Momentum](gemgreen)
- [ ] Util: [Frostblink](gemblue), [Blood & Sand](gemred), [Intimidating Cry](gemred)

- [ ] Link 2: [Leap Slam](gemred)+[Momentum](gemgreen)

### Level 12: Mervail
- [ ] Link 1: [Sunder](gemred)+[Ruthless](gemred)+[Chance to Bleed](gemred) or [Momentum](gemgreen)
- [ ] Link 2: [Leap Slam](gemred)+[Faster Attacks](gemgreen)+[Momentum](gemgreen)

</ul>

  - For Righteous Fire look for <a href="b">BBB</a><a href="r">R</a> links.
  - For Fire Trap look for <a href="g">GGG</a><a href="r">R</a> links.

## <span id="respec"></span>LEVEL 52 RESPEC (BA)
### Time at BA: 2h30, Level 52

<div class="note-block">
<ul class="custom-list bullet-list">

- Use Campaign respec points first (~ 14 pts)
- Can get 16 pts if you do Fetid Pool
- Spend gold to finish the respec according to tree below
- Remove points in <a href="r">Red</a> and take <a href="g">Green</a> on tree below.
  
</ul>
</div>
<img src="327_sunder/images/final.png">

<div class="note-block">
<ul class="custom-list bullet-list">

- 26 point respec require d= 16 from campaign + 10 from gold
  - 10 pts @ 313 gold each (lvl 52) = 3130 gold
  - In a test run (lvl 52 @ BA) I ended with 7282 gold after doing 3 armor gambles at level 24 in act 3 to get 1 4-link.
  - After 26 point respect I ended with 4152 gold which I used to gamble other armor pieces or sceptre/shield for RF swap.
- Gamble body (705 gold), gloves/helm (676 gold) for 4-link RF/FT colors if you did not get from quest rewards in A6 or A7.
- As a reminder these are the colors you're looking for:
  - Righteous Fire - <a href="b">BBB</a><a href="r">R</a>
  - Fire Trap - <a href="g">GGG</a><a href="r">R</a>
  - Curse - <a href="b">BBB</a><a href="r">R</a>
  - Movement - <a href="g">GG</a><a href="r">R</a><a href="b">B</a>
  
</ul>
</div>

## <span id="rfheist"></span>RF HEIST LINKS
Try to have these links as soon as you get to BA and start doing heists. You can swap around the auras to fit your needs. For example, if you think you're tanky enough or if you have enough damage, feel free to replace determination or malevolence with [Haste](gemgreen) so you can move faster.
<div class="link-block" id="rfheist">
<ul class="checklist">

- [ ] Link 1: [RF](gemblue)+[Elemental Focus](gemblue)+[Lifetap (max)](gemred)+[Burning Damage](gemred) or [Efficacy](gemblue)
- [ ] Link 2: [Fire Trap](gemgreen)+[Swift Affliction](gemgreen)+[Lifetap (max)](gemred)+[Trap & Mine Damage](gemgreen)
- [ ] Link 3: [Shield Charge](gemred)+[Lifetap (1)](gemred)+[Faster Attacks](gemgreen)+[Momentum](gemgreen)
- [ ] Link 4: [Vitality](gemred)+[Arrogance](gemred)
- [ ] Link 5: [Punishment](gemred)+[Lifetap (1)](gemred)+[Enduring Cry](gemred)+[Frostblink (1)](gemblue)
- [ ] Aura: [Determination](gemred), [Malevolence](gemblue), [Skitterbots](gemblue)
- [ ] Util: [Blood Rage](gemgreen)

</ul>
</div>

## <span id="rfmap"></span>RF MAPPING LINKS
- The RF gems are in order of importance, some gems like Swift Affliction will not work without the use of Lifetap as it adds a duration tag.
- It will be important to buy aura gems directly from A3 so they start at Level 11. Getting Purity of Fire and Determination first is really big for survivability. 
- If you have enough damage or defences, you can use Haste to move faster.
<div class="link-block" id="rfmap">
<ul class="checklist">

- [ ] Link 1: [RF](gemblue)+[Burn Damage](gemred)+[Ele Focus](gemblue)+[Efficacy](gemblue)+[Lifetap (max)](gemred)+[Swift Aff](gemgreen)
- [ ] Link 2: [Fire Trap](gemgreen)+[Swift Affliction](gemgreen)+[Lifetap (max)](gemred)+[Trap & Mine Damage](gemgreen)
- [ ] Link 3: [Shield Charge](gemred)+[Lifetap (1)](gemred)+[Faster Attacks](gemgreen)+[Frostblink (1)](gemblue)
- [ ] Link 4: [Vitality](gemred)+[Arrogance](gemred)
- [ ] Link 5: [Punishment](gemred)+[Lifetap (1)](gemred)+[Enduring Cry](gemred)
- [ ] Aura: [Determination](gemred), [Purity of Fire, ](gemred)[Malevolence](gemblue) or [Skitterbots](gemblue)
