Melee Leveling

  • Axe recipe: Axe + magic/rare/ unique + Blacksmith's Whetstone
    • Axe Progression: Use 1.30 APS or 1.25 APS if no other option
    • Stone (1) > Woodsplitter (13) > Poleaxe (18) > Gilded (28) > Headsman (45)
  • Silo map has fire dmg%/ignite chance hybrid craft for RF
