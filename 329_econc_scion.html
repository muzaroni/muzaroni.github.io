ACT 1 PROGRESSION

    • Starting Gems: Spectral Throw+Prismatic Burst
    • To make transition into Act 2 smooth, try to have GGG & GGR by level 12 (end of Act 1)
    • Below are all breakpoints by monster, after each name go to town and get the items listed.

Tarkleigh

    • Get Splitting Steel(right gem) → Replace Spectral Throw for Mud Flats ONLY
    • Do not discard Spectral Throw
    • Buy Corroded Blade
    • Buy shield, ideally with GR link for Shield Charge+Faster Attacks

Hailrake

    • Get Quicksilver
    • Look for links (see below) for options. GGR feels best, but GGG sets you up till Act 3
    • GGR = Spectral Throw+Chance to Bleed+Volley or Momentum
    • GGB = Spectral Throw+Prismatic Burst+Volley or Momentum
    • GBR = Spectral Throw+Prismatic Burst+Chance to Bleed
    • GGG = Spectral Throw+Volley+Momentum
    • Buy Amber Amulet / Iron Ring 3x
    • Get Volley

Dweller

    • Get Decoy Totem
    • Buy Frostblink
    • Buy Sniper's Mark
    • Buy Momentum or Chance to Bleed
    • Buy the other gem you didn't get earlier

Brutus

    • Get Faster Attacks
    • Get Withering Step
    • Buy Amber Amulet / Heavy Belt 3x

Fairgraves (Enter Caverns)

    • Get Poisonous Concoction

    Level 1-11: Use Spectral Throw

    • Link 1: Spectral Throw+Volley+Chance to Bleed
    • Link 2: Sheild Charge+Faster Attacks+Momentum
    • Aura: Precision (1)
    • Util: Frostblink, Withering Step, Sniper's Mark

    Level 12: Swap to Poisonous Concoction

    • Link 1: Poisonous Concoction+Volley+Momentum

    Total Cost: 5+ Wisdoms

ACT 2 PROGRESSION

Fidelitas

    • Get Herald of Ash
    • Buy Blood Rage
    • Buy Amber Amulet / Heavy Belt 3x

Weaver

    • Get Elemental Damage with Attacks

Bandits: Alira

    • Buy Lesser Multiple Projectiles from Act 1, level off hand
    • Link 1: Poisonous Concoction+Volley+LMP or EDWA
    • Link 2: Sheild Charge+Faster Attacks+Momentum
    • Aura: Precision (1), Herald of Ash
    • Util: Frostblink, Withering Step, Sniper's Mark, Blood Rage

    Total Cost: 1 Alteration , 3 Wisdoms
