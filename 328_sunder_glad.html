3.28 [Gladiator] Sunder Bleed Leveling

    • This guide is a leveling guide for basic Sunder Bleed Glad with the intent of swapping into CWS Glad for GGG Ultimatums.

ACT 1 PROGRESSION

End of Act 1: 20-23 mins - Level 12

    • Starting Gems: Double Strike+Chance to Bleed
    • Ensure you buy G+R linked linked somewhere before leaving town

Tarkleigh

    • Buy Spectral Throw this will be your main skill for all of Act 1
    • Buy any G+Rlinked
    • Pick up any shield and put it on off-hand for Shield Charge at level 4

Hailrake

    • Get Quicksilver
    • Get RuthlessorVolley
    • This is based on if you can find links RRG or GGR
    • Buy Momentum
    • Get Shield Charge
    • Buy Lapis Amulet / Iron Ring 3x
    • Buy Frostblink

Dweller

    • Get Faster Attacks

Brutus

    • Get Leap Slam
    • Buy Intimidating Cry

Fairgraves (Enter Caverns)

    • Buy Sunder

    Level 1-11: Use Spectral Throw

    • Link 1: Spectral Throw+Chance to Bleed+Ruthless or Momentum

    Level 10: Replace Shield Charge with Leap Slam

    • Link 2: Leap Slam+Momentum

    Level 12: Mervail

    • Link 1: Sunder+Chance to Bleed+Ruthless or Faster Attacks
    • Link 2: Shield Charge+Momentum+Faster Attacks
    • Util: Frostblink, Blood & Sand, Intimidating Cry

    Total Cost: 2 Transmute, 3+ Wisdoms

ACT 2 PROGRESSION

End of Act 2: 38-40 mins, Level 20

Fidelitas

    • Get Herald of Ash
    • Buy Blood Rage
    • Buy Herald of Purity
    • Buy Lapis Amulet3x

Weaver

    • Get Cruelty
    • Link 1: Sunder+Chance to Bleed+Cruelty or Faster Attacks
    • Link 2: Leap Slam+Faster Attacks+Momentum
    • Aura: Herald of Ash,Herald of Purity
    • Util: Frostblink, Intimidating Cry, Blood Rage

    Total Cost: 2 Alterations , 3 Wisdoms

ACT 3 PROGRESSION

End of Act 3: 55-60 mins, Level 28-30

You can gamble at Maramoa at level 24 for 4 Links. Focus on armor since it's used for a longer time than weapons which you should always be upgrading. DO NOT OVER SPEND as you will need gold later to respec to RF in Act 9
    • 2H Axe / body armor: 145 gold each
    • Gloves / Helmet / Boots: 135 gold each < BETTER OPTION

Piety

    • Get Pride
    • Buy Vulnerability
    • Link 1: Sunder+Chance to Bleed+Cruelty or Faster Attacks
    • Link 2: Leap Slam+Faster Attacks+Momentum
    • Aura: Herald of Ash,Pride
    • Util: Frostblink, Intimidating Cry, Blood Rage, Vulnerability

    Total Cost: 1 Alteration
