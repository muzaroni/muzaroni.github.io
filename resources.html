Resources

Updated: Feb 11, 2026

This might seem like a long list but I practically use every single one of these multiple times a league. Some tools/websites I probably could not give up when playing POE1.

Most Used 3rd Party Tools

    • Path of Building: This is the real game
    • Awakened POE Trade: Defactor POE1 price checker, can also make useful chat macros.
    • Filterblade: Defacto loot filter generator. Highly customizable, just use this, I've tried them all this is straight up the GOAT.
    • Chaos Recipe Enhancer: Chaos recipe tool. Highlights items in stash. Incredibly useful for league start.

Browser Extensions

    • Better POE Trading: Basically a bookmark system for all your complex trade searches
    • POE Fuzzy Search: This adds "~" in the search boxes, makes it MUCH faster to find mods and items

Most Used Websites

    • PoE.Ninja: Single best place to research builds and market prices.
    • PoE.Watch: Market price tracker similar to ninja.
    • PoEDB: Very useful for finding modifiers and their tiers on every single item.
    • WealthyExile: Currency tracker. Great way to see if you're a shit mapper or blaster
    • Craft of Exile: Similar to PoEDB but different UI. Also has craft simulator thats very useful.
    • POEPlanner: Plan your Atlas tree or Passive tree in browser.
    • Path of Regex: Best website to make REGEX.
    • Disenchant Tool: Find which items are good to disenchant for Kingsmarch.
    • Timeless Calculator: Find timeless jewels.
    • Vorici Calculator: Find if its cheaper to chrome or to use jeweller orb to color/socket.
    • Cluster Jewel Calculator: Easy way to find Large clusters where you don't take the middle notable.
    • Hideout Showcase: Find ways to spend money on this game.

Good Creator Content

    • Team Focus YouTube - Arguably the best videos for learning group speedrunning.
      • Their Group Running POE 101 and Routing a Run videos are the big standouts.
    • LLYD - 5-10 min Act 1 Layout tutorials
    • Cyclon - Excellent layout breakdowns of every zone. Would recommend if you want to get faster at campaign
    • Tuadh - Posts a ton of 2 stone run vids. Incredible resource for league start.
